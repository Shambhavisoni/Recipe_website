Carrot Cake

Carrot cake is a moist and indulgent dessert made with grated carrots, warm spices, and a rich cream cheese frosting. The carrots add sweetness and moisture to the cake, while spices like cinnamon and nutmeg infuse it with warmth and depth of flavor. Topped with creamy frosting and sometimes garnished with nuts or coconut, carrot cake is a beloved treat for any occasion.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min

servings

6 servings

Tags : Beef Breakfast carrots food

instructions

Step 1

In a mixing bowl, combine grated carrots, flour, sugar, oil, eggs, and spices. Mix until well combined.

Step 2

Heat a non-stick skillet and melt butter. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and cook, stirring gently until they reach the desired consistency.

Step 3

Allow the cake to cool completely, then frost with cream cheese frosting and serve.
