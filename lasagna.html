Spinach And Mushroom Lasagna

Spinach mushroom lasagna is a delicious and comforting pasta dish layered with lasagna noodles, sautéed mushrooms, spinach, creamy ricotta cheese, and tangy marinara sauce. It is a vegetarian-friendly option that doesn't compromise on flavor. The lasagna noodles are cooked until tender, then layered with a combination of sautéed mushrooms and spinach. The ricotta cheese adds a creamy element, while the marinara sauce provides a tangy and robust flavor. Baked to perfection, the lasagna is served hot, with each bite offering a delightful combination of textures and flavors.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min

servings

6 servings

Tags : Beef Breakfast carrots food

instructions

Step 1

Sauté mushrooms and spinach in a skillet until tender.

Step 2

In a baking dish, layer lasagna noodles, marinara sauce, sautéed mushrooms and spinach, and ricotta cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.

Step 3

Bake in the oven until the lasagna is bubbling and the top is golden brown. Allow it to rest before serving.

ingredients

Lasagna noodles

mushrooms

spinach
