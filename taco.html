Beef Tacos

Beef tacos are a delicious Mexican dish consisting of seasoned ground beef, served in warm tortillas and topped with a variety of garnishes. The flavorful beef is cooked with spices such as cumin, chili powder, and garlic, resulting in a savory filling. Topped with shredded cheese, lettuce, salsa, and sour cream, these tacos offer a delightful combination of textures and flavors.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min

servings

6 servings

Tags : Beef Breakfast carrots food

instructions

Step 1

Brown the ground beef in a skillet, breaking it into crumbles.

Step 2

Add taco seasoning and water, simmer until the beef is cooked and flavors meld.

Step 3

Warm tortillas and fill them with the seasoned beef, topping with desired condiments like shredded cheese, lettuce, salsa, and sour cream.

ingredients

Ground beef

taco seasoning

condiments (such as shredded cheese, lettuce, salsa, sour cream).

tools

Skillet

tortilla warmer

serving platter

spatula
