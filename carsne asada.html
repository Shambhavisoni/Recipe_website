Carsne Asada

Carsne asada is a flavorful dish loved by food enthusiasts. Tender, marinated beef with spices creates a mouthwatering experience. Grilled to perfection, it boasts a smoky char and juicy tenderness. Toppings like cilantro, onions, and lime add refreshing contrast. Enjoy it at gatherings or street food markets worldwide. Savor the deliciousness of carsne asada tacos or plates.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min

servings

6 servings

Tags : Beef Breakfast carrots food

instructions

Step 1

Marinate the beef in a flavorful mixture.

Step 2

Grill or sear the marinated beef until cooked to your desired doneness.

Step 3

Allow the beef to rest briefly, then slice it into thin strips.

ingredients

Beef (preferably flank or skirt steak)

Marinade ingredients (such as lime juice, garlic, cilantro, cumin, chili powder, salt, and pepper)

Toppings and garnishes (such as fresh cilantro, diced onions, and lime juice)

tools

Grill

Skillet

Cutting Board

Resealable Plastic Bag
