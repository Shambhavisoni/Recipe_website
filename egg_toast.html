Scrambled Eggs with Toast

Scrambled eggs with toast is a classic breakfast dish that never fails to satisfy. Light and fluffy scrambled eggs, seasoned with salt and pepper, are cooked to perfection and served alongside crispy toast. This simple yet comforting combination is a staple on breakfast tables, providing a protein-packed start to the day.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min

servings

6 servings

Tags : Beef Breakfast carrots food

instructions

Step 1

Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk with salt and pepper.

Step 2

Heat a non-stick skillet and melt butter. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet and cook, stirring gently until they reach the desired consistency.

Step 3

Toast bread slices and serve them alongside the scrambled eggs.

ingredients

Eggs

salt

bread slices

tools

Non-stick skillet

whisk for beating the eggs

spatula

toaster
