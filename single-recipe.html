Banana Pancakes

Banana pancakes are a delightful breakfast treat that combines the sweetness of ripe bananas with fluffy pancake batter. To make them, mashed ripe bananas are added to the pancake batter, resulting in a naturally sweet and moist texture. The pancakes are cooked on a griddle or pan until golden brown and then served warm. They are often enjoyed with a drizzle of maple syrup, a sprinkle of cinnamon, or a dollop of whipped cream for extra indulgence. Banana pancakes are a great way to use up overripe bananas and create a deliciously satisfying morning meal.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min

servings

6 servings

Tags : Beef Breakfast carrots food

instructions

Step 1

Start by mashing ripe bananas in a bowl until they become smooth.

Step 2

In a separate mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas with pancake mix or homemade pancake batter.

Step 3

Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.

ingredients

1 1/2 cups dry pancake mix

1/2 cup flax seed meal

1 cup skim milk

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
