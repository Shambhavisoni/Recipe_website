Greek Ribs

Greek ribs are a delectable dish featuring succulent pork ribs infused with the vibrant flavors of Greek cuisine. The ribs are marinated in a mixture of olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, and Mediterranean spices, ensuring a delightful blend of tangy and herbaceous notes. Grilled or oven-baked to perfection, they acquire a crispy exterior while remaining tender and juicy on the inside. Greek ribs are often served alongside classic Greek sides like tzatziki sauce, lemon potatoes, and a fresh salad, providing a complete and memorable dining experience that showcases the essence of Greek flavors.
