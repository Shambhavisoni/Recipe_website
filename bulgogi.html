Beef Bulgogi

Beef Bulgogi is a Korean dish featuring thinly sliced marinated beef that is sweet, savory, and packed with flavor. It is often enjoyed with rice and accompanied by various side dishes. The beef is marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger, then grilled or stir-fried to perfection. The result is tender, juicy beef with a caramelized exterior, complemented by the vibrant flavors of the marinade. Whether served in lettuce wraps or with a side of kimchi, Beef Bulgogi is a mouthwatering and satisfying culinary experience.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min

servings

6 servings

Tags : Beef Breakfast carrots food

instructions

Step 1

Prepare the marinade by combining soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, garlic, and ginger.

Step 2

Slice the beef thinly and marinate it in the sauce for at least 1 hour.

Step 3

Cook the marinated beef in a hot skillet or grill until caramelized and cooked through.

ingredients

Thinly sliced beef (such as sirloin or ribeye)

soy sauce

sesame oil

tools

Skillet or grill for cooking the beef

tongs for flipping the meat

knife for slicing the beef

bowl for mixing the marinade
