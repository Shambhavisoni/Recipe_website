Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish featuring al dente spaghetti noodles smothered in a rich and hearty meat sauce. The sauce is made with a combination of ground beef, aromatic vegetables, tomatoes, herbs, and spices, creating a flavorful and satisfying meal. Topped with grated Parmesan cheese and served with garlic bread, this dish is a favorite comfort food enjoyed worldwide. The long-simmered sauce develops deep flavors, while the spaghetti noodles provide the perfect vehicle for soaking up every bit of deliciousness. Spaghetti Bolognese is a timeless classic that satisfies cravings for a hearty and comforting Italian meal.

prep time

30 min

cook time

15 min

servings

6 servings

Tags : Beef Breakfast carrots food

instructions

Step 1

In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and saut√© diced onions, minced garlic, and chopped carrots until softened.

Step 2

Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Stir in tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, dried herbs (such as oregano and basil), salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

Step 3

Cook spaghetti noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain the noodles and serve with the Bolognese sauce. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh basil, if desired.

ingredients

Ground beef

Diced onions

Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes

tools

Large skillet or saucepan for cooking the sauce

Wooden spoon or spatula for stirring the ingredients

Pot for boiling the spaghetti noodles

Colander for draining the cooked noodles
